Top 5 Chess Books for Improving Tactics (Beginners & Intermediate)

Chess tactics are essential for improving your game. Winning material through pins, forks and skewers, or even checkmating! Tactics can instantly turn the game around in your favor. Chess books are an amazing way to improve! Check out this top 5 of chess books to get better at chess tactics, and be ready to beat anyone on the chess board!

5.Back to Basics: Tactics by Dan Heisman

This chess book provides a clear, structured approach to tactical training. Heisman’s book focuses on the essential motifs: forks, pins, skewers, and basic mating combinations. With accessible explanations and practice exercises, it helps beginners develop confidence in spotting tactics and applying them in real games.
